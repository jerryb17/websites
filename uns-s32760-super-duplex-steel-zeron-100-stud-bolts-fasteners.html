Super Duplex S32760 Stud Bolts Manufacturer, Zeron 100 Screw Exporter.

Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading manufacturer of UNS S32760 Fasteners In India. Zeron 100 Fastener are high-alloy steels. Super Duplex Zeron 100 Bolts are available in four groups that include martensitic, austenitic, ferritic and precipitation-hardened steels. Zeron 100 Stud contain larger amounts of chromium in comparison with other steels and thus have good corrosion resistance. UNS S32760 Stud Bolts contain about 26% of chromium. Zeron 100 Threaded Rod is a super duplex steel whose design enables combining improved resistance to pitting, high strength, stress corrosion, crevice corrosion and cracking.

Zeron 100 Nuts contains super duplex steel resists sulfide stress corrosion and chloride environments. The carbon ranges are 0.03% maximum for the Super Duplex UNS S32760 Stud Bolts. most of the applications that employ grade uns s32760 are replaced by low-cost duplex steel grade 2205. We are most versatile and widely supplier of Zeron 100 Screws. The benefits of Our Super Duplex Fasteners include a long service life that will retain an attractive, clean finish with low maintenance cost.
