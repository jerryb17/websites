Stainless Steel Fasteners Grades

We are stainless steel fasteners distributors of following types :

304 Stainless Steel: 18-8 (304) stainless steel contain 18% chromium and 8% nickel. This is the most common stainless Steel Fasteners Material.

316 Stainless Steel: A highly corrosion resistant grade of stainless steel and also known as marine fastener grade of stainless steel. Ideal for salty water and chlorine environments. More expensive but durable than SS 304 (18-8).

410 Stainless Steel: A stainless alloy that is harder than 304 stainless steel, but not as resistant to corrosion as 18-8 (304) and 18-8-3 (316).
