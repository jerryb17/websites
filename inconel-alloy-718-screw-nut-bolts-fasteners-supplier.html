Inconel 718 fasteners are used in a large number of industries and applications all over the globe as they have excellent rupture, fatigue, tensile and creep strength. Due to these characteristics, inconel 718 threaded rod are used in applications and areas such as land-based gas turbine engines, casings, liquid fuel rockets components, cryogenic tankage, rings, sheet metal parts for aircraft, etc. The Reduction of Area of inconel 718 studs is 15% at Room Temperature and at 1200 °F it is also 15%. Inconel 718 stud bolts are ideal for use in down-hole shafting and high strength bolting.

Inconel 718 nuts, as well as inconel 718 set screws are used in instrumentation parts and as fasteners. The Chemical Composition of inconel 718 bolts is C 0.08% max, P 0.0015% max, Ni 50.00 – 55.00%, Nb 4.75 – 5.50%, Al 0.20 – 0.80%, Cr 17.00 – 21.00%, Mo 2.80 – 3.30%, Cu 0.30% max, Co 1.00% max, Si 0.35% max, Mn 0.35% max, S 0.0015% max, B 0.006% max, Ti 0.65 – 1.15% and Fe balance. UNS N07718 bolting is manufactured from nickel-chromium alloy, which is a precipitation-hardenable alloy and this contains considerable quantities of molybdenum, niobium and iron and also smaller quantities of titanium and aluminum. Due to these characteristics, inconel 718 hex bolts are used in various applications and industries such as nuclear reactors, gas turbines, spacecraft, tooling, rocket motors, pumps, etc.

At room temperature, inconel 718 washers have a Tensile Strength of 185 ksi and at 1200 °F it is 145 ksi. Inconel 718 screws have a Yield Strength of 150 ksi at Room Temperature and at 1200 °F it is 125 ksi. Inconel 718 stud bolts are made from nickel-chromium and thus they are highly resistant to corrosion and as they and inconel 718 socket head cap screws also exhibit high strength, they can be used at extreme temperatures ranging from -423 °F to 1300 °F. Alloy 718 bolts have superb weldability, they exhibit high strength, they are resistant to postweld cracking and they also display excellent resistance to corrosion. Due to these properties, alloy 718 bolts are used in element spacers in nuclear fuels, rocket motors, jet engines, cryogenic storage tanks, hot extrusion tooling, gas turbine components, parts and bodies of pumps, thrust reversers, etc.
