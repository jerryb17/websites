Stainless Steel T Nuts, T Nut Manufacturer.

316 Stainless T Nut Exporter in Mumbai, M8 T Slot Nut Suppliers.

Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading T Nut Manufacturer India. Stainless Steel T Nut is a very high end austenitic stainless steel. 316 Stainless Steel T Nut is available in four groups that include martensitic, austenitic, ferritic and precipitation-hardened steels. M8 T Slot Nut is the most versatile nut.

M8 T Nut contain higher grip compare to other steels and thus have good corrosion resistance. 316 SS T Nuts have high strengh grip than any other normal nuts.

304 Stainless Steel T Nut is a high strength nut whose design enables combining improved resistance to pitting, high strength, stress corrosion, crevice corrosion and cracking. We Are most versatile and widely stockist of M12 T Nuts. The benefits of our nuts include a long service life that will retain an attractive, clean finish with low maintenance cost.
