Weld Neck Flanges also known as a high-hub weld neck flange and tapered hub weld neck flange is a type of flange. There are two designs. The regular type of Stainless Steel Weld Neck Flange is used with pipes. The Long Weld Neck Flange is unsuitable for pipes and is used in process plant. Carbon Steel Weld Neck Flange consists of a circular fitting with a protruding rim around the circumference. Generally machined from a forging, these flanges are typically butt welded to a pipe. The rim of a WNRF Flange has a series of drilled holes that permit the flange to be affixed to another flange with bolts.

Reducing Weld Neck Flange is suitable for use in hostile environments that have extremes of temperature, pressure or other sources of stress. The resilience of 150 LB Weld Neck Flange is achieved by sharing the environmental stress with the pipe with which it is welded. Weld Neck Raised Face Flange has been used successfully at pressures up to 5,000 psi.
