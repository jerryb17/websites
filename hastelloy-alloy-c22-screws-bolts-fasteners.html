Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading manufacturer of Hastelloy C22 fasteners which display superb resistance to a huge range of chemical processenvirons, including the likes of acetic anhydride, hot contaminated organic media, cupric chloride, acetic acid, ferric chloride, brine solution, hot contaminated inorganic media, seawater, formic acid and chlorine. Alloy C22 bolts can be used in the as-welded condition, for most chemical applications. The Typical Ultimate Tensile Strength of hastelloy C22 studs is 758 MPa (110 ksi) and the Typical 0.2% hastelloy C22 bolt yield strength is 379 MPa (55 ksi).

As hastelloy C22 screws show superb resistance to chloride ions and sulfur compounds, they are used in flue gas desulfurization systems. Hastelloy C22 hex bolts are manufactured from Hastelloy C22 which is a wrought alloy of chromium-nickel- molybdenum and which is considered to be an alloy that is ‘versatile corrosion resistant’. The Chemical Composition of hastelloy C22 bolts is Cr 16, Si 0.08, C 0.01, Ni a 57, Fe 5, Co 2.5, Mn 1, W 4, Mo 16 and others V-0.35. Alloy C22 fasteners fare superbly good in various kinds of industrial applications, more so in chemical processing. Hastelloy C22 washer display adaptability and as such they can be used in problematic zones.

Hastelloy C22 socket head cap screws exhibit superb resistance to corrosion in reducing media, as well in oxidizing media. Hastelloy C22 nuts display fabulous resistance to attacks from localized corrosion. As hastelloy C22 stud bolts are extremely versatile, it is possible to use them in multipurpose plants or where there is the possibility of the occurrence of ‘upset’ conditions. Hastelloy C22 hex bolts display brilliant resistance to stress-corrosion cracking and pitting. Hastelloy C22 set screws have the capacity to resist the corrosive effects of chlorine dioxide, hypochlorite and wet chlorine gas.
