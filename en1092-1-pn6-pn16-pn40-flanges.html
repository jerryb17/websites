Regal Sales Corporation are manufacturer of EN 1092 Flange. EN 1092-1 is the European standard for flanges. This European Standard for flanges was approved by CEN on July 6 th , 2001. This standard specifies the requirements of circular steel flanges in PN designations from PN 2.5 - PN 400 and in nominal sizes ranging from DN 10 - DN 4000. Specified by this standard are various types of flanges and their face surface finishes, tolerances, masses, threading, materials, facings, markings, temperature ratings, dimensions, pressure ratings, etc. Flanges are used for connecting pumps, pipes and valves and various other equipment and in this way, a piping system is formed.

Flanges give very convenient access for the purposes of inspection, cleaning and modification. Flanges can be screwed or welded. Flanged joints can be created by using a gasket between them, so as to form a seal and bolting two flanges together. EN 1092-1 flanges consist only of DIN based flanges. These types of flanges are famous for their superb performance and they are developed for meeting various specific demands. There are diverse kinds of flanges, some of which start with the prefix ‘PN’, such as EN 1092-1 PN6 flange or for example other kinds of flanges called EN 1092-1 PN40 flanges. In these types of flanges, the PN means ‘Pressure Nominale’ and it denotes the pressure rating of the flange. For instance a PN25 flange has been designed to operate up to a 25 bar. Another type of this flange is EN 1092 PN16.

EN 1092-1 Type 11 also known as EN 1092 Type 11 flange, are flanges are available in various materials such as Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Stainless / Duplex Steel, Nickel Alloys / Super-alloys, Titanium, Aluminium, Copper, Polypropylene etc. EN 1092-1 threaded flange is akin to slip-on flanges, but the bore of the threaded pipe flange consist of threads that are tapered. There is no welding needed to attach these EN 1092-1 threaded flanges. It is possible to use these EN 1092-1 threaded flanges with piping that consists of external threads. EN 1092-1 threaded flanges are used in applications that involve high pressure usage.

EN 1092-1 material is defined by one of the primary reference legislations that govern systems that use steel, in particular stainless steel and this reference legislation is none other than EN 1092-1. EN 1092-1 is responsible for defining and regulation of the production and identification of flat face flanges that are used for the joining of accessories, valves and pipes. EN 1092-1 loose flange is also known as a Lap Joint Flange and this consists of two parts. It looks like a slip-on flange or a weld neck flange and it is butt welded with the pipes. EN 1092-1 loose flanges are used in the kind of systems that are frequently dismantled for the purposes of cleaning and inspection. These various types of flanges are used in different industries such as oil and gas, water supply, chemicals, food processing, power plants, plumbing, heating, pulp and paper, fabrication, etc.

  • Standard:EN 1092-1 Flange.
  • Dimensions : ANSI B16.5, DIN Series A & B, MSS SP44, ASA, API-605, AWWA, Custom Drawings
  • Size : 1/2" (15 NB) to 48" (1200NB)
  • Class : 150 LBS, 300 LBS, 600 LBS, 900 LBS, 1500 LBS, 2500 LBS, DIN Standard ND-6,10, 16, 25, 40 Etc.
  • Flange Face Type : Flat Face (FF), Raised Face (RF), Ring Type Joint (RTJ)

Material & Grades of EN 1092-1 Flanges :

Carbon Steel : ASTM A 105 (high temperature carbon steel), ASTM A350 LF1 to LF3 (low temperature carbon steel), ASTM A694 Gr. F42 / F52 / F56 / F60 / F65 (high yield carbon steel flange to match API 5L linepipes)

Alloy Steel : ASTM A182 Gr. F1 / F2 / F5 / F9 / F11 Cl. 2 / F12 Cl. 2 / F22 Cl. 3 / F91 (alloy steel flanges)

Stainless Steel EN 1092-1 Flange : ASTM A182 F 304/304L, 316/316L, 321, 347, 348 (stainless steel flanges), 904/904L

Duplex Steel EN 1092-1 Flange : ASTM A182 F51 (duplex flanges) / F53-F55 (super duplex)

Nickel Alloys / Super Alloys EN 1092-1 Flange : ASTM B166 UNS NO6600 (inconel 600) ASTM B564 UNS N06625 (Inconel 625), ASTM B425 UNS-NO8800 (Incoloy 800), ASTM B564 UNS N08825 (Incoloy 825), ASTM B160 UNS N0200 (Nickel 200), ASTM B564 UNS N04400 (Monel 400), ASTM B564 UNS N10276 (Hastelloy C-276)
