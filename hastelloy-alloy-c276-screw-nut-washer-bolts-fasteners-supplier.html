Hastelloy C276 Bolts

Hastelloy C276 Fasteners are nickel-molybdenum-chromium superalloy with an addition of tungsten designed to have excellent corrosion resistance in a wide range of severe environments. Hastelloy C276 Bolts have high nickel and molybdenum contents make the nickel steel alloy especially resistant to pitting and crevice corrosion in reducing environments while chromium conveys resistance to oxidizing media. Hastelloy C276 Stud Bolts have low carbon content minimizes carbide precipitation during welding to maintain corrosion resistance in as-welded structures. This Hastelloy C276 Screws are resistant to the formation of grain boundary precipitates in the weld heat-affected zone, thus making it suitable for most chemical process application in an as welded condition.

Although there are several variations of the Hastelloy nickel alloy Fasteners, C-276 Hastelloy Fasteners is by far the most widely used. Alloy C276 Bolts are widely used in the most severe environments such as chemical processing, pollution control, pulp and paper production, industrial and municipal waste treatment, and recovery of sour natural gas.

Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading manufacturer of Hastelloy C276 fasteners which display superb resistance to a huge range of chemical processenvirons, including the likes of acetic anhydride, hot contaminated organic media, cupric chloride, acetic acid, ferric chloride, brine solution, hot contaminated inorganic media, seawater, formic acid and chlorine. Alloy C276 bolts can be used in the as-welded condition, for most chemical applications. The Typical Ultimate Tensile Strength of hastelloy C276 studs is 758 MPa (110 ksi) and the Typical 0.2% hastelloy C276 bolt yield strength is 379 MPa (55 ksi).

As hastelloy C276 screws show superb resistance to chloride ions and sulfur compounds, they are used in flue gas desulfurization systems. Hastelloy C276 hex bolts are manufactured from Hastelloy C276 which is a wrought alloy of chromium-nickel- molybdenum and which is considered to be an alloy that is ‘versatile corrosion resistant’. The Chemical Composition of hastelloy C276 bolts is Cr 16, Si 0.08, C 0.01, Ni a 57, Fe 5, Co 2.5, Mn 1, W 4, Mo 16 and others V-0.35. Alloy C276 fasteners fare superbly good in various kinds of industrial applications, more so in chemical processing. Hastelloy C276 washer display adaptability and as such they can be used in problematic zones.

Hastelloy C276 socket head cap screws exhibit superb resistance to corrosion in reducing media, as well in oxidizing media. Hastelloy C276 nuts display fabulous resistance to attacks from localized corrosion. As hastelloy C276 stud bolts are extremely versatile, it is possible to use them in multipurpose plants or where there is the possibility of the occurrence of ‘upset’ conditions. Hastelloy C276 hex bolts display brilliant resistance to stress-corrosion cracking and pitting. Hastelloy C276 set screws have the capacity to resist the corrosive effects of chlorine dioxide, hypochlorite and wet chlorine gas.
