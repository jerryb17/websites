Stainless Steel Spectacle Blind Flange is basically made up of two stainless steel discs attached to each other by a metal strip. One is solid stainless steel disc and the other is hollowed disc. The hollowed stainless steel disc allows fluid flow and solid disc is used for positive isolation. We can simply rotate a SS Spectacle Blind Flange by 180 degrees to close or open a pipe to allow fluid to pass.

Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading Supplier of Carbon Steel Spectacle Blind Flange which are generally applied to permanently separating pipesystems, or just to connect with each other. ASTM A105 Spectacle Blind Flange is a steel plate cut into two discs of a certain thickness.

The two discs of ASME B16.48 Spectacle Blind Flange are attached to each other by section of steel similar to the nose piece of a pair of glasses. PN16 Spectacle Blind Flange be applied in systems, which regularly need to be separated from other installations. Normally, ASME B16.5 Spectacle Blind Flange is mounted in the "open" position so that flow through the pipe is possible. If the Spectacle Flange in the "close" position is rotated, the pipe is blanked off then flow of liquid is impossible to pass.
