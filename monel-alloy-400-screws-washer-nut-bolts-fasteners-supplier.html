Monel 400 Fasteners are manufactured from an alloy of nickel-copper. As such, Alloy 400 fasteners display superb toughness and very high strength. Monel 400 Bolts have UNS N04400 (QQ-N-281). Monel 400 hex nuts are in big demand all over the globe in various industrial processes and applications as they are alloys of nickel-copper and they not only exhibit very high strength, but they are also highly resistant to corrosion in various environs such as pure water, sulfuric acid, non-oxidizing mineral acids, sea water, hydrofluoric acid, salts and alkalis. Because of the high resistance to corrosion and the superb mechanical properties displayed by it at cryogenic temperatures, Monel 400 threaded rod is used in many applications and industries such as oil & gas industry, pumps, marine industry, valves, etc.

Because of their characteristics, Monel 400 socket head cap screws are used to a great extent in marine engineering, chemical and hydrocarbon processing applications and also in applications and industries that deal in hydrocarbon processing. Alloy 400 washer display excellent ductility. Monel 400 stud has almost a non-magnetic tensile strength and a minimum of 82,000 psi. Over a wide range of temperatures, Monel 400 nuts, show excellent resistance to corrosion. Monel 400 bolt properties are that of Chemical composition of Fe max 2.5%, Si max 0.5%, Ni + Co min 63%, C max 0.3%, S max 0.024%, Cu min 28% and max 34% and Mn max 2%.

A very outstanding feature of Monel 400 bolts and nuts is that they display superb resistance to hydrofluoric acid in all concentrations, even at its boiling point. This is a really excellent feature that is displayed by Monel 400 screws, as hydrofluoric acid is a very tough acid to deal with. Fact is, in many industries where hydrofluoric acid is used, alloy 400 bolts are the most commonly used of the entire specialty alloys, as they appear to be the most resistant. At high temperatures, Monel 400 bolts display excellent toughness, as well as high strength. Due to the intrinsic characteristics exhibited by Monel, it has to be worked slowly.

In reducing environs, Monel 400 fasteners, exhibit quite a good resistance to hydrochloric acid and sulfuric acid and in marine environs, they display very superior resistance to corrosion and this is why they and also Monel 400 washers, are used a lot in applications that involve chemical processing and marine applications. From subzero temperatures, to temperatures up to 480 °C, Monel exhibits excellent mechanical properties. Monel work-hardens rapidly. Monel 400 hex bolt are covered with a coat of Monel and this is what makes them resistant to corrosion and heat. This coating of Monel, also gives these fasteners resistance to discoloration from heat and protection against salt water. For all your needs of fasteners of Monel, be sure to call us.
