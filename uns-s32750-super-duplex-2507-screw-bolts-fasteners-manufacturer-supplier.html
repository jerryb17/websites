Super Duplex Fasteners

Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading manufacturers of Super Duplex Fasteners. Super Duplex 2507 Bolts are high-alloy steels. 2507 Super Duplex Bolts and Nuts are available in four groups that include martensitic, austenitic, ferritic and precipitation-hardened steels. Super Duplex Screws contain larger amounts of chromium in comparison with other steels and thus have great amount of corrosion resistance. Super Duplex 2507 Anchor Bolts contain about 24% of chromium. Super Duplex Studs is a made up of super duplex steel whose design enables combining improved resistance to pitting, high strength, stress corrosion, crevice corrosion and cracking.

Super Duplex Steel 2507 Nuts contains super duplex steel resists sulfide stress corrosion and chloride environments. The carbon ranges are 0.03% maximum for the ASTM A479 UNS S32750 Stud Bolts. Most of the applications that employ grade 2507 are replaced by Duplex Steel 2205 Fasteners. Super Duplex Anchor Bolts have very high mechanical strength and properties. Because of physical properties that offer design advantages, we manufacture different types of super duplex washers in India. Super Duplex 2507 Bolts also known as UNS S32750 Bolts in engeenering companies and aerospace department. S32750 Super Duplex Threaded Rod has 25% chromium (25Cr Duplex), 4% molybdenum and 7% nickel also known as X2CrNiMoN25.
