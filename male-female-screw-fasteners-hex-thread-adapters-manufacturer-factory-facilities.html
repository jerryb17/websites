Regal Sales Corporation was founded in 2004 as a exporter of Industrial Steel Fasteners,now Regal Sales become a professional manufacturer of male female screw fasteners,male female threaded fasteners,male-female hex thread adapters,high-strength fastener Stud Bolts, Nuts Washers, Screws, Threaded Rod, Dowel Pins, U-bolts, J-bolts, Eye-bolts, Anchor-bolts, Hex Head Bolts, Round Bent U-bolts in black, HDG (Hot Dipped galvanized steel ), Zinc Plated, 304 SST (stainless steel), 316 SST (stainless steel) and Polyolefin Coated. It covers an area of 40000 square meters with total assets of 3 million USD, the annual capacity more than 15000 metric tons.

Our production series are screws and bolts, including standard of ANSI & DIN & BS & ISO diameter range is from 1/4” to 2”, Specialised in Titanium Grade 2 & 5 & 8 etc. And the diameter range of metric bolts is from M6 to M50, the class is 4.8 & 6.8 & 8.8 & 10.9 etc. Surface treatment are including black, zinc plated, hot dip galvanized, mechanical zinc plated, phosphate Etc.

60 multi-stage cold headers are installed in our Factory, 3 sets of continuous carburizing quenching furnaces and many other auxiliary equipments. The inspection equipments include tensile strength tester, carbon & sulphur analyzer , rockwell and vicker hardness tester, metallographic analyzer etc. All of these machines are operated by trained staff having experience more than 10 years.

Regal Sales provides manufacturing and distribution of custom and standard Bolts.
Distributor of Industrial Steel fasteners Included in Following Material : A-286 corrosion resistant nickel; Inconel®, stainless steel; lock, square & steel nuts; A286, stainless steel & wrench sockets,Monel, A286 corrosion resistant nickel, Inconel®, MP35N, C276, 321 SST (stainless steel), 310 SST (stainless steel), 316/316L SST (stainless steel), 304/304L SST (stainless steel), 410 SST (stainless steel), 347 SST (stainless steel), aluminium 6061T6, & naval brass.

We have Cold Heading capabilities include multi-station headers, nut formers and parts formers. which enable us to manufacture a wide range of specialty male and female fasteners.

Services :

  • Raw Material : Pickling, Drawing, Annealing.
  • Heat Treatment : Oil, Quench Austemper.
  • Cold Forging : Nut Formers (4 to 7 Stations), Parts Formers (4 to 7 Stations), Bolt Forners (1 Die/1 Blow to 4 Die/4 Blow).
  • Surface Finish : Plating, Coating.
  • Tapping : Thread Rolling, Tapping.
  • Cleaning : Washing, Degreasing.
  • Cold Chamber Die Casting : 125 Tons to 1,200 Tons.
  • Hot Chamber Die Casting : 30 Tons to 500 Tons.
  • Equipment Features : Vacuum assist available on all machines, Hot Oil used for heating / cooling dies, Automatic Extractors and Ladles, All machines are computer monitored, In-house machine shops to maintain tooling.
  • Alloys Poured : Aluminum, Zinc, Magnesium.
  • Equipment : CNC Lathes, CNC Vertical Mills, Custom-Built Multi-Spindle Machining Centers.
  • Stamping : Press sizes range from 30 Tons to 1,000 Tons.
  • Assembly : Automated and semi-automated staking, welding, tapping and assembly operations.
  • Other Services : Laser and Vision Sorting, Automated and Semi-Automated Assembly, Packaging, Custom Labeling and Barcoding.
