Hex Bolt
Hex Head Bolt
Hex Head Shoulder Bolt

A hex bolt has various names and these include hex cap bolt, hexagon screw head bolt, machine bolt and hex-cap screws. Hex bolt have six sided heads. Hex head bolts are used a lot in repair and construction. These bolts have hexagonal heads and they also have machine threads and they are used in a tapped hole or with a nut. As hex shoulder bolts are used in large number of industries and applications all over the world, these types of bolt are made from various kinds of materials, even mild steel hex bolt.

Reference to M6 hex bolt and another kind of bolt known as M8 hex bolt, the ‘M’ denotes the bolt is of metric size. In the metric bolt specification for bolt, the first number is with reference to the nominal outer diameter of the bolt. For example, if a bolt has been specified as ‘M6-1.0 x 30’, this would mean that this bolt would fit a hole that has a diameter of 6.0 mm. One of the biggest benefits of using a stainless steel hex bolt is that these kinds of bolt are resistant to corrosion. Plus, they are also resistant to very cold temperatures and they also resist high heat. But that’s not all, hex bolt made of stainless steel are environment friendly, they have a superb life cycle and they are also 100% recyclable.

A carbon steel hex bolt is available in various grades. Hex bolt of low or medium carbon steel are cold worked and they fall in Grade 2 and Grade A307. Hex bolts of medium carbon steel are quenched and tempered and they fall under Grade 5. Hex bolt of medium carbon alloy steel are also quenched and tempered and these come in Grade 8. Hex flange bolt have a circular flange under their head. This acts as a washer and distributes the load. Non-serrated flange bolt are also known as frame bolt or hex flange screws and these are basically hex bolt with a one-piece hex-washer and they have a flat bearing surface.

Hex socket bolts are a kind of bolt that has a hexagon socket/hexagonal drive hole and a compact cylindrical head. These bolts are threaded right up to their head. These bolts are also called socket screw, socket head capscrew or Allen bolt and they are a kind of cap screw. Hex head shoulder bolts are frequently used as pivot points. These bolt allow different machine parts, metals, wood and various applications, to assemble, rotate and slide over and they can also be used as a kind of stopping mechanism.
