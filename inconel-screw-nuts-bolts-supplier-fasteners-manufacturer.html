Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading Inconel Fasteners Manufacturers. The thermal stability of inconel fasteners is very high and this is why it is possible to use them and Inconel Washers and Inconel Bolts Suppliers which are in service temperatures that are in the range of cryogenic to 982°C (2200°F). In environs that are mild, such as the likes of neutral salts, atmosphere, alkaline media and sea water, M8 inconel studs face as good as zero attack. Inconel bolts, as well as inconel spring washers, have very good toughness and they have high strength. Inconel hex cap screws display temperature stability and they are highly resistant to corrosion. Due to these characteristics, inconel studs, as well as inconel set screws, are the perfect material for use in industries such as oil & gas, marine, chemical processing, electronics, aerospace, etc.

Inconel socket head cap screws and inconel nord lock washers are in big demand in various industries and applications, as they exhibit very good resistance to corrosion and at elevated temperatures they display very high strength. M10 inconel studs and also inconel cap screws have high alloy content and due to this, they have the capability of withstanding a huge range of environs that are extremely harsh.

Inconel screws, as well as M6 inconel bolts, belong to a family of alloys of molybdenum-chromium-nickel. The high content of molybdenum and nickel in m10 inconel studs, makes them resistant to environs that are nonoxidizing. In environs that are highly corrosive, the mix of chromium and nickel, makes inconel studs and nuts resistant to oxidizing chemicals. Because of these properties, inconel threaded rod, as well as inconel flat washers, are used in industries such as power-generation, petrochemical, etc.
