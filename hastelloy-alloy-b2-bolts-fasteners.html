Regal Sales Corporation is one of the leading manufacturer of Hastelloy B2 Fasteners In India. Hastelloy B2 Bolts are high-alloy steels. Hastelloy B2 Hex Bolts are available in four groups that include martensitic, austenitic, ferritic and precipitation-hardened steels. Type Hastelloy B2 Stud Bolts contain larger amounts of nickel in comparison with other steels and thus have good corrosion resistance. Alloy B2 Screws contain about 68% of nickel. B2 Hastelloy Threaded Rod is a hastelloy whose design enables combining improved resistance to pitting, high strength, stress corrosion, crevice corrosion and cracking and Hastelloy B2 Lock Nuts is mostly used in the fasteners family.

Hastelloy B2 Tie Bar contains stainless steel resists sulfide stress corrosion and chloride environments. The carbon ranges are 0.02% maximum for the Hastelloy B2 Set Screws. We are most versatile and widely supplier of B2 Hastelloy Washers.
