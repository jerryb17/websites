Stainless Steel Socket Head Cap Screw, Standard Socket Head Cap Screw Manufacturer.

Socket Head Cap Screw Exporter in Mumbai, Socket Head Cap Screw Suppliers.

Regal Sales Corporation is one of the specialized Socket Head Cap Screw Manufacturers in India. Socket head cap screws are not grade designated as are hex head cap screws. A standard inch series socket head cap screw is 20% stronger than a Grade 8 hex head cap screw and 50% stronger than a Grade 5 hex cap screw. The tensile strength is a minimum 180,000 psi for sizes 1/2" diameter and smaller and 170,000 psi for 5/8" diameter and above. Socket Head cap screw with Low head, flat head and button head cap screws which are rated 10,000 psi lower due to the head configuration and are seen more in lighter duty applications. Stainless Steel Socket Head Cap Screw is a non-stabilized alloy steel with low carbon content. Flat Socket Head Cap Screws is the most common screw. Titanium Socket Head Cap Screw is high alloy steel to improve products resistance against strong reducing acids, such as sulphuric acid. SS Socket Head Cap Screw is also resistant to stress corrosion cracking and crevice corrosion.

Grade 12.9 Socket Head Cap Screws is the most versatile and the most widely used of all alloy steels screws. We Are most versatile and widely supplier of Standard Socket Head Cap Screws. The benefits of Our Screws include a long service life that will retain an attractive, clean finish with low maintenance cost.
