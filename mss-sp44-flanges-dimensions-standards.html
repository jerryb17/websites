MSS SP-44 Flanges Manufacturer.

MSS SP 44 Blind Flanges Suppliers, MSS SP 44 Slip on Flange Exporter in Mumbai.

The American National Standard developed and published by the Manufacturers Standardization Society (MSS) of the Valve and Fittings Inc., MSS SP-44 Flange Standard practice covers pressure-temperature ratings, materials, dimensions, tolerances, marking, and testing for steel pipeline flanges. In accordance with the document, the welding neck type flanges are to be forged steel, while the blind flanges can be made from either forged steel or steel plate.
