Stainless Steel Machine Screw Manufacturer in India

Regal Sales Corporation is a largest Stainless Steel Screws Manufacturers in India and Stainless Steel Roofing Screws Suppliers. We make two most commonly use stainless steel screws types such as Stainless Steel 304 Screw also known as 'A2 Screw' and SS 316 Screws also known as 'A4 Screw' and both of these grades offer great corrosion resistance, scrubbed ability and the ability to handle major low and high temperature environment.

Stainless Steel Screws are type of a fasteners and in some ways it is homogeneous to bolts and its characterizes by a helical ridge also known as male external thread. Stainless Steel Machine Screws has a round head which are used to join sheet metal parts using hex or square nuts whether Stainless Steel Set Screw has no head meaning that the screw is fully threaded and it it used to secure an object against another object, mostly without the nut.

SS Self Tapping Screw have a tip and threaded pattern and also available with various types of screw head, thus it can tap its own hole as it is driven into the product or material which has to fasten and prevent pull out. there are many types of screws who help to fasten the materials such as Stainless Steel Socket Head Cap Screw commonly used in assemblies that do not provide sufficient clearance for a conventional wrench or socket and Stainless Steel Marine Screw which are made up of stainless steel 316 which provide great corrosion resistance in salty water or in low temperature environment, Stainless Steel Deck Screw is alike drywall screw but its improve corrosion resistance and its supplied in a large gauge, they have bugle heads which allows to depress the wood surface without breaking the screw and Stainless Steel Wood Screw is a metal screw with a sharp point designed to attach two pieces of wood together and they are mainly use in wood.
